Headquartered in Dublin, Ireland, Trane Inc., a member of the Ingersoll Rand family of brands, is a world leader in air conditioning systems, services and solutions.

Moscow Kremlin, New York’s Grand Central Station, the Burj Khalifa tower in Dubai and La Scala Opera House in Milan are equipped with Trane energy-efficient heating, ventilating and air conditioning systems.

Trane specialists designed and installed Europe's largest cooling system with 480 kilometers of water cooling pipes in the 50.45 km. long Channel Tunnel beneath the English Channel that links the United Kingdom and France.

RTD-16.2 full height turnstiles with RTC-16 canopies are installed at the Société Trane plant located in Charmes, Vosges department, France.

RTD-16.2 full height turnstile is designed for facilities with high requirements for maximum safety and security. It can operate in gateway mode.

RTC-16 canopy provides additional protection from downfalls and prevents climbing over the turnstile.

Employees use contactless cards to access the territory.

Installer: ITcotation
