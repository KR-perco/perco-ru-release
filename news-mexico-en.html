In November, PERCo equipment was presented at Intertraffic, the international trade show for infrastructure, safety, parking, smart mobility and traffic.

PERCo partner in Mexico, RODHE Security, exhibited the following equipment at its booth:

  • ST-01 Speed gate
  • TTD-08A Box tripod turnstile
  • TTR-08A Tripod turnstile

The exhibition revealed a keen interest of visitors in ST-01 speed gate, which meets the current market demand for contactless access control.

The speed gate was equipped with a face recognition terminal with fever and face mask detection
