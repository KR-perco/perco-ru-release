========================= Описание для анонса =========================

Speed gates (turnstiles with swing or sliding panels) are designed to organize checkpoints with increased requirements for design and convenient passage. The turnstile posts аre made of glass and stainless steel.

ST-01 Speed gates with swing panels can be equipped with three versions of top covers made of glass, stainless steel, or tempered glass with stainless steel inserts. There are versions of ST-01 Speed gates with a built-in card capture reader and a barcode scanner.

On request, it is possible to implement various modifications of ST-01 Speed gate, such as plexiglass and polycarbonate swing panels, front panels, and brackets for additional equipment (face recognition terminals, readers, pyrometers, or other versions for the customer purposes).

Readers and controllers can be installed under the top covers of the ST-02 Speed gates with sliding panels. The turnstile top covers are made of tempered glass.

ST-11 Speed gates with swing panels are distinguished by compact design, which allows installing them in limited spaces.
